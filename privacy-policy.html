Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use the CHK (Chithode Happy Kids) application.

1. Permissions & Data Collection

Camera Permission

Our app requires access to your device's Camera. This permission is used solely for the purpose of taking photos for student profiles or scanning QR codes within the app. We do not record video or take photos without your direct action, and we do not store or transmit any camera data to our servers unless explicitly uploaded by you.

Personal Data

We do not collect any personal data automatically. Any information you enter (like student names or attendance) is stored securely and used only for the educational management of the school.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for app functionality. You can read their privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for educational use. We do not collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 without parental/school consent.
